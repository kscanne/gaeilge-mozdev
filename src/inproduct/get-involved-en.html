Contributing to the Mozilla Europe project and the Mozilla Project

Almost every person and every European organisation can contribute to the Mozilla Europe project. Whether you are an individual, or represent a company, an administration or a University, your contribution can take many forms:

For individuals: become a member

You can become a member of Mozilla Europe Association, to give a little amount of money to finance our effort. When you join, you will receive a superb collector's item T-shirt, designed exclusively for Mozilla Europe Members.

For individuals: make a donation

If you would like to help Mozilla Europe to promote development and usage of the Mozilla browser and its byproducts, please send a check (in Euros) to our snail mail address:

Mozilla Europe
28 rue Viala
75015 PARIS
FRANCE

If you already have a Paypal account, you can use it to donate some money to Mozilla Europe.

We also encourage you to donate to the Mozilla Foundation, based in the US.

For companies and administrations

Companies or administrations willing to financially support Mozilla Europe's efforts can reach us via e-mail: corporate-sponsor@mozilla-europe.org.

For Universities and engineering schools

Past students-contributors to the Mozilla project have developed an invaluable educational experience. Mozilla Europe is interested in partnering with Universities, Engineering schools and other Computer Science education entities to have students contribute to the Mozilla project while studying. Teachers and students of such Engineering schools and Universities should contact Mozilla Europe via e-mail: academia@mozilla-europe.org

Contributing to the Mozilla.org project

To contribute to the Mozilla source code, please refer to the Mozilla.org Web site.
